About us

Our main focus is the customer and always trying to come up with new ideas to make the customers experience more enjoyable and we hope we are doing just that, so if you are ever in Galway please feel free to drop in and let us know how we are getting on!

We take pride in looking after our beers, the Guinness is regularly touted by reviewers and customers alike as the best in Galway, the atmosphere is friendly and welcoming and we would put our bar staff up against the best of them.
